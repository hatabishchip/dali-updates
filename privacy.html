DALI Dictation - Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Overview

DALI Dictation ("the App") does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer.

Voice Data

The App records your voice locally and sends it to the Deepgram API for transcription. This is done using your own Deepgram API key, which you provide in the App's settings. The developer has no access to your audio recordings or transcriptions.

Data Storage

All data (API keys, transcription history) is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to or stored on developer servers.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the Deepgram API for speech recognition. Your use of Deepgram is governed by Deepgram's Privacy Policy.
